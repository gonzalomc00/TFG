Instructions

How to play

Go to the main menu to play a game. If you are not there, just clic on the CChase button in the navigation bar and you will be redirected. Once you are there, choose a gamemode and hit the Play button.

Game modes

Single Player Mode

In this mode you must answer 10 questions.

To answer a question, you must fill in each ‘square’ with a letter that completes the answer. If you answer correctly, you will win 10 points.

A timer will tell you the time you have to provide an answer for each question.

If time runs out before you answer, you will not win any points and will lose a life.

You have 5 lives available in this mode. You can use one life to skip questions that you are unable to answer, but you will not win any points if you do so.

Once you have used up all your lives, if you provide an incorrect answer, you will be dropped from the game and lose.

Scoring 100 points earns you a gold medal, 90 points earn you a silver medal and 80-70 points earn you a bronze medal.

Classroom Challenge Mode

In this mode you will take part in a class competition. To begin the game, you must enter the code given by your lecturer.

To answer a question, you must fill in each ‘square’ with a letter that completes the answer. If you answer correctly, you will win 10 points.

A timer will tell you the time you have available to read each question and provide the correct answer. If time runs out before you answer, you will not win any points.

In this game mode, you can win extra points the faster you are. The seconds you have not used when answering the question will be added to your score. That is, if you answer a question correctly when there were 30 seconds left, 40 total points will be added to your score. Answer as many questions as fast as you can to win more points. The student with the most points by the end of the game will win a gold trophy

Infinite Mode

In this mode, you must answer as many questions as you can.

To answer a question, you must fill in each ‘square’ with a letter that completes the answer. If you answer correctly, you will win 1 point.

A timer will indicate the time available to read and then answer the question.

There are only 2 lives in this mode. Once you have used up these lives, if you provide an incorrect answer, you will be dropped from the game and lose.

My profile

You can click on your profile picture in the navigation bar to access to your profile. There you can see all the achievements and trophies you have earned. Your History is also located in this page. Here you can see all the games you have played in the last 90 days. By clicking in one entry of the History you can have access to all the information about that game.

Leaderboards

You can check the leaderboards at any moment. There you will see three diferent rankings and the Top 10 players in each category. Compete to be one of them!
